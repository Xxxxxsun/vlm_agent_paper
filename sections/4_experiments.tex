\subsection{Experimental Setup}
\label{sec:setup}

We evaluate \sysname{} on four visual reasoning benchmarks: \textbf{ChartQA}~\citep{masry2022chartqa},
\textbf{MathVista}~\citep{lu2023mathvista}, \textbf{HRBench}~\citep{chen2024hrbench}, and
\textbf{V$^*$}~\citep{wu2024vstar}.
All experiments use a training subset of $k{=}200$ cases and $N{=}3$ evolution iterations unless
stated otherwise.
Full implementation details (hyperparameters, prompt templates, hardware) are in
Appendix~\ref{sec:impl_details}.

% ============================================================
